Dotmod.

  • Case Study
  • Dotmod

Overview.

Dotmod is top 5% US vape industry hardware manufacturer brand, that since our campaigns and rebranding has secured them a great market share, internationally and domestically. When we began, Dotmod was having issues - with bad blood from previous partners and poor brand coherency being the main two obstacles they faced. They were locked out of their social media accounts and lost a lot of their following and lost market share and trust from the fallout. This being the case, Dotmod’s plan went from trying to reclaim the accounts to rebuilding the brand from scratch. They wanted to be sleek and sexy, so we rebuilt everything from the ground up and made them appear as high quality as their products are.

The Objective.

Improve the sense of unity across all their products, brand, and community while utilizing a scarcity strategy to boost the exclusivity of each product drop. We rebranded everything from their website to the packaging.

Project Approach:
  • SOCIAL MEDIA + INFLUENCER TEAM STRATEGY
    CONTESTS + ACTIVATIONS

    Conducted monthly giveaways for Facebook engagement and growth across USA and French markets.

    INFLUENCERS

    Influencer Team campaign model provided a value of ~$1.00 USD to 1 follower ratio on Instagram distributed across the cost of paying for promotional product (evaluated at retail) and the top 10 influencer monthly payouts.

    DIGITAL STRATEGY

    Built out, executed and maintained a detailed digital strategy with a healthy balance of fan engagement and influencer / photographer resulting in $250,000 in additional sales via the online channels. Continual renewing of this plan is keeping their growth strong, and winning awards, even after we had left.

    PRODUCT RELEASES

    We assisted dotmod with over 20+ product launches and releases, utilizing attention grabbing "drip campaign" methods to create exclusivity and scarcity.

  • COMPANY REBRAND
    WEBPAGE

    Redesigned webpage, improved its content organization, and improved product visibility resulting in greatly increased Direct-To-Customer sales.

    PACKAGING

    Mocked up packaging, manuals, and changed naming convention to funnel into the identification of the main brand identity. (Ex. "Petri" mod changed to "the dotMod, or dotBox", etc.).

    MEDIA + CONTENT

    Improved graphic design, photography, video, and supporting documents for all sales and marketing efforts.

    SALES + PROMO MATERIALS

    All product catalogs, UI/UX, promotional products and were developed, approved, and distributed through online and tradeshow channels. (Including SWAG -- HATS, Interactive documents, videos)

  • COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT
    Social Channel Community Management

    We were responsible for managing all social media accounts and their communities. Providing customer service support and directing customers to products.

    UGC CAMPAIGNS

    Created UGC call-to-action campaigns to increase visibility of contests, posts, etc. This gamified and increased the communities sense of camaraderie and connection with each other and the brand.

    PUBLIC RELATIONS

    Oversaw and consulted on two large PR scandals and salvaged a mishandling of client by a prior sales team. These efforts mitigated great financial and social loss.

    COMMUNITY BUILDING

    We Networked with industry specific groups and got them to cosponsor events, engage in posting cross-promotional content, and incentized their communities to convert and buy.


    • Key Results Summary.

    Dotmod was named Best Product of 2017, 2018 @ NVE, VapExpo Paris in 2019 and other events. We Created a digestible and effective influencer Marketing Strategy that coordinated product launches with well known vape Influencers, Photographers, events, and pages for a manageable budget.

    KPIs:
    +900% Follower Increase
    From 10K to 100k+ on Instagram.
    +$400k Sales Increase
    in additional online sales gross.
    +2 Million Total
    social media impressions per month.
    6% AVG. Engagement Rate
    3% hougher than industry standard.
